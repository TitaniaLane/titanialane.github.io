About Ghost Kitchen

Ghost Kitchen is an Online Multiplayer Steam game with up to 16 friends, taking on the role of either Chef or Butcher. Chefs win by completing orders flawlessly or uncovering the butchers and voting them out of the kitchen. Butchers aim to sabotage orders and turn innocent chefs into mincemeat. Keep things fresh with 4 unique maps and 2 different food types to choose from.
